About Us

For our CECS senior project we wanted to create something to help teach people about sustainable living. Climate change is a hot topic of debate in todayâ€™s politics. Although scientists have shown through thousands of studies that man made climate change is real, there are those who still deny it. Some of these individuals have the authority to make great strides in the race against climate change. However, they instead choose to dismantle any progress made towards achieving global sustainability. Therefore, the protection of our planet and environment has been left to us.

According to the Japanese Plastic Waste Management Institute, at 77 percent, Japan's recycling rate is over 3 times as much as the rate in the US (20 percent). Japanese recycling culture is something that is instilled from a young age. However, in the US, the country that creates the most waste in the world, a major issue with sustainability is a lack of education. In order to educate people, we wanted to create an Amazon Alexa skill that will provide information to users about how to reduce their global impact. We hope to educate others and spread the idea that many small incremental changes will one day lead to great progress.
